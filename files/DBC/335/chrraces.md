### Flags
<code>Col: 1 (uint32)</code>

| Value | Flag | Name | Comment |
|-------|------|------|---------|
| 1 | 0x01 | CHRRACES_FLAGS_NOT_PLAYABLE | NPC only |
| 2 | 0x02 | CHRRACES_FLAGS_BARE_FEET | Do Not Component Feet |
| 4 | 0x04 | CHRRACES_FLAGS_CAN_MOUNT | Can Mount |
