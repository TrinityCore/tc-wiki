### ID <!-- {#id-alt} -->

### LocaleMask
:x: <code>Col: 1 (uint32)</code>

&nbsp;

### CharsetMask
:x: <code>Col: 2 (uint32)</code>

* 1: Western
* 4: Cyrillic script
* 10: Hangul (Korean glyphs)
* 17: Mandarin (Chinese/Taiwanese glyphs)
